How staffing works on Hyra

Add your group

Get your group onto Hyra. Simply input your Group's ID, and the role of your Medium Ranks+. Hyra will automatically import your team.

Wait for the data

Install the Hyra Activity Tracking script in your Group games and watch your team's activity data flow in. See who's playing and who's performing the best in realtime.

Review

Take action based on the data we receive. Typically most group's do a review every week, but you can do these whenever you like. Then distribute and repeat!
